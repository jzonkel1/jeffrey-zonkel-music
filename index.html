The Story So Far

A South Texas
Songster

JEFFREY ZONKEL is a singer-songwriter out of Corpus Christi, Texas, playing what he calls oldtime psychedelic country blues — fingerpicked guitar, harmonica on a rack, and a voice that sounds like it drifted in off a 78 rpm record.

His songbook runs from originals like Guitar Man, People, and Baby I'm Movin' In to the deep well of Doc Watson, Dock Boggs, Hank Williams, Jimmie Rodgers, and Townes Van Zandt. For the better part of a year he busked the Downtown Corpus Christi Artwalk nearly every month, guitar case open on the sidewalk — and held down a monthly residency at the Packery Bar & Grill for a year straight, with one-off nights everywhere from museum galleries to a breakfast joint that had never once hosted live music.

His debut single, Hush Little Baby, is out now on every platform — with more on the way. He's not chasing the gig circuit these days, but for the right room, the case still opens.
